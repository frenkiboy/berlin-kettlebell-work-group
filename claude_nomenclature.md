# Kettlebell Juggling: Complete Taxonomy & Progression System

## Existing Systems in the Field

Three major systematized approaches to kettlebell juggling exist:

**Logan Christopher** (Legendary Strength / kettlebelljuggling.com) created the most formal ranking system with 20 levels and a Master certification.
